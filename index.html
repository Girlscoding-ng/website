Girlscoding is aimed at girls in tech. The project is a non-profit that aims to increase diversity in tech by making role models more visible.“Data proves young women in the world develop an interest in STEM subjects at 11 and a half years old. We have four to five years to nurture that interest before girls self-select out of STEM.” According to recent studies, girls lose interest in STEM (science, technology, engineering, math) already at age 15. Female role models are crucial to changing this, as well as providing girls with the opportunity to express their creativity through hands-on experiences. We aim to increase the number of of girls in tech in the world as a whole.

FRONT END DEVELOPMENT

Front-end web development is the practice of converting data to a graphical interface, through the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, so that users can view and interact with that data

BACK END DEVELOPMENT

Back end of a website consists of a server, an application, and a database. A back-end developer builds and maintains the technology that powers those components which, together, enable the user-facing side of the website to even exist in the first place.
