    ABOUT

    GirlsCoding is a Non-governmental that is very passionate about the Girl child and the education of the Girl child. We are passionate about increasing the number of females in STEM, training the girls in underserved community and improving the resourcefulness of young girls and women through our widespread packages. GirlsCoding empowers young ones at school with our well seasoned short exciting courses and STEM projects. Girlscoding is focused on using structured STEM curriculum to train young ones. GirlsCoding enriches the underserved girls with strong IT skills and improve their resourcefulness.
